What is Facebook?

Facebook is a social networking platform that allows users to connect with friends, family, and acquaintances, share updates, photos, videos, and engage in various activities such as liking, commenting, and sharing content.

Key Features of Facebook

  • Profile and Timeline
  • News Feed
  • Friend Requests and Connections
  • Groups and Events
  • Messaging and Chat
  • Privacy Settings
  • Pages and Business Tools

History of Facebook

Facebook was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates. Initially, it was limited to Harvard University but later expanded to other colleges and eventually to the general public. Over the years, Facebook has grown to become one of the largest social media platforms with billions of users worldwide.
